## 🟪 My Playground: Where a Student Teaches Code to Think

This isn’t your average student portfolio.  
I’m Kunj, a learner obsessed with building AI agents that learn, adapt, and sometimes surprise me.  
I believe the best way to understand AI is to unleash it in the wild—so my repos are experiments, playgrounds, and sometimes, little anomalies.

- 🦾 **I’m a student first, developer second, and an AI explorer always.**
- 🤖 **My goal?**  
  To create agents that can code, collaborate, and even challenge me.
- 🧑‍🎓 **Why?**  
  Because the future of learning is collaborative, and my best study buddies are… well, not always human.
